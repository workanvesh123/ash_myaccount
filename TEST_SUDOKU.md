# 🔢 Test Sudoku Now!

## ⚡ Quick Test (3 minutes)

### Step 1: Navigate to Sudoku
1. Go to http://localhost:4200/games
2. Click the **Sudoku** card (🔢 icon)

### Step 2: Start Easy Game
1. Select **Easy** difficulty
2. Click **Start Game**
3. See the 9×9 grid with some numbers filled

### Step 3: Play the Game
1. **Click an empty cell** (it will highlight blue)
2. **Click a number** (1-9) on the number pad below
3. **Watch the cell fill** with your number
4. **Try wrong number** - See it turn red, mistakes counter goes up
5. **Try correct number** - Cell stays normal

### Step 4: Try Features
1. **Notes Mode:**
   - Click "✏️ Notes" button
   - Click a cell
   - Click numbers to add pencil marks
   - Click again to remove

2. **Hint:**
   - Click "💡 Hint" button
   - Watch it fill a cell for you
   - You have 3 hints total

3. **Clear:**
   - Select a cell with your number
   - Click "🗑️ Clear" button
   - Cell becomes empty

### Step 5: Complete Puzzle
- Fill all cells correctly
- See completion modal with your stats!
